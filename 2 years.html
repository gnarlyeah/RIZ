2 Years Max

Fair lady, I pray thee stay
While I fix my hair and clothes,
For I would not have you see me
In a state that's rough and coarse.

I know not how long it will take
For me to make myself presentable,
But I hope that you will wait
For me to be suitable.

I promise that I will be worth it,
For I know that you are worth the wait,
And when I am done and ready,
I'll make sure that our date is great.

So please, dear lady, do not go,
But wait for me to be ready,
For I cannot bear the thought
Of being without you, sweet and steady.

I'll make it up to you, I swear,
For the time that you have spent,
For you are the one I hold dear,
And I hope that our love will never end.
