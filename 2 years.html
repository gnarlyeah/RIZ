2 Years Max

Ladies fair, and gentle maidens all,
I pray you, hear my humble call:
For I, a man, am broken now
And seek to fix my shattered brow

I know that I am far from whole
And lack the grace and charm you know
But if you'll stay and wait a while
I'll work to fix my broken style

I'll mend my heart and soul with care
And strive to be more fair and fair
I'll seek to be the man you need
And earn your love and trust, indeed

So if you'll stay and wait with me
I'll work to set my spirit free
And when I'm whole and strong again
I'll love you true, my dearest friend

Ladies fair, and gentle maidens all,
I pray you, hear my humble call.
