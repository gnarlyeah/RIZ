Bahalag E-who you ko nmo inig balik ^_^

    Oh sweetest love, most fair and pure,
    Thou art the one who holds my heart,
    I cannot hide these feelings more,
    But must confess and play my part.
    
    For when I see thy angel face,
    My soul doth stir with pure delight,
    And when thou speakest with such grace,
    My spirit takes to new heights.
    
    I know not how to win thy love,
    But I will try with all my might,
    For thou art worth more than all the stars above,
    And I will love thee with all my might.
    
    So let me tell thee of my love,
    And hope that thou wilt understand,
    I am thine, forevermore,
    Thy faithful lover and devoted hand.
    
    Oh sweetest love, most fair and true,
    Please grant me but a chance,
    To love and cherish thee,
    And dance the dance of romance.
